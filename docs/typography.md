### Default Typography

```js
const Typography = require('@material-ui/core/Typography').default;
<div>
    <Typography>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Typography>
    <Typography>1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9 0 ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ</Typography>
</div>
```
